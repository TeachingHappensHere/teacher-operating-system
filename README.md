### Included
- Stable direct Assessments & Data route
- Student-profile connection
- Beginning-, middle-, and end-of-year screening windows
- Weekly assessment records
- DIBELS, ORF, accuracy, Maze, NWF, Open Court, Eureka, writing, science, and social studies types
- Score formats and benchmark targets
- Complete, scheduled, in-progress, and make-up statuses
- Screening checklist generation
- Missing-assessment tracking
- Intervention progress-note connection
- Search and filters
- Local JSON backup export
- Dashboard, Student Data, Intervention, and Health connections
- Responsive desktop, iPad, and phone layouts

### Test after deployment
1. Open Students and confirm sample profiles exist.
2. Open Assessments & Data.
3. Choose Build Screening Checklist.
4. Add or edit a sample score.
5. Mark an assessment complete.
6. Test student, window, type, and status filters.
7. Export a local backup.
8. Confirm the page remains stable.
