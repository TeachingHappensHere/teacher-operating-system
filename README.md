### Important

The Great Minds portal may require the school’s authorized login. Version 8.2 links to the portal and stores your planning notes locally; it does not copy or redistribute Great Minds curriculum content.

### After deployment

1. Hard-refresh the site.
2. Open **Eureka Math²**.
3. Confirm Module 1 is selected.
4. Enter the current lesson number and lesson title.
5. Add the objective, materials, lesson components, and exit ticket.
6. Plan the 20-minute Math 2 follow-up.
7. Send the lesson to Weekly Planning and Teach My Day.
8. Test the Great Minds portal button while signed into the school account.
