Teacher Operating System v23.1.0
Student Accommodations Engine

WHAT CHANGED
- Added Micah G.'s active IEP classroom accommodations profile.
- Added an "Apply Micah G. IEP Supports" button beneath the IEP field in the Subject Planner.
- The generated supports adjust automatically for reading, MOWR, Heggerty, writing, and math lessons.
- Universal classroom and assessment accommodations are included in the generated lesson-plan text.
- Updated offline caching and build metadata.

HOW TO USE
1. Open Lesson Plans.
2. Choose the subject and day.
3. Open section 3: Differentiation / Accommodations.
4. Select "Apply Micah G. IEP Supports."
5. Review the inserted text and adjust only when the lesson requires a more specific support.

PRIVACY
The accommodations control appears only in teacher planning screens. It is not added to the classroom display.
