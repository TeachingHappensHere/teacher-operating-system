Open Court • Unit 1 • Lesson 1

The Mice Who Lived in a Shoe

Model lesson hub for reading, phonics, vocabulary, grammar, writing, assessment, intervention, and Teacher Brain notes.

🎯 Objective

Students will read and discuss the story, use vocabulary in context, practice phonics patterns, identify common and proper nouns, and answer cause-and-effect comprehension questions.
