    Open Court • Unit 1 • Lesson 1

    The Mice Who Lived in a Shoe

    Reusable model for future lesson workspaces.

    🎯 Objective

    Students will read and discuss the story, use vocabulary in context, practice target phonics patterns, identify common/proper nouns, and answer cause-and-effect questions.

    🔤 Phonics

    /ch/, /th/, /sh/, /wh/, /ar/

    📝 Vocabulary

    admired, fetched, gathered, huddled, merchant, observed, platform, safety, shelter, timber

    📐 Grammar

    Common and proper nouns

    💜 Teach It Like Mrs. Parrish

    • Teach vocabulary before reading.
    • Spend extra time on merchant.
    • Use partner talk before cause/effect questions.
    • Do not skip the Open Court packet.
